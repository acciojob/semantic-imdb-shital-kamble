Kaho Na Pyarr Hai is a 2000 indian Hindi-language romantic action film written, directed and produced by Rakesh Roshan. it makes the debuts of his son Hrithik Roshan(in a double role) and actress Ameesha Patel.

Trivia

The movie was added to the Guiness Book of Word Records 2002 edition for winnning the most number of awards for a movie. Kaho Na Pyarr Hai won a total of 92 awards.
